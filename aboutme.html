Accomplishments

2021

Started my Bachelor's in Finance at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley. I also joined a student organization called Enactus. At 18 years old, I led a team to feed 1,600 families across 3 counties, and raised over 28 thousand dollars.

2022

This year, I began to explore the full college life. I presented (in front of 1,000 people! And placed 2nd in the nation) for an early stage project with Enactus. I also ran as senator for UTRGV's College of Business where I acted as chair for the finance committee. Leading a team of 10 senators to manage an $80,000 budget.

2023

In 2023, I again led a team to feed 1,000 families for Thanksgiving, raising over $25,000. This year, I also began working at Knapp Community Care Foundation, here, I conducted quantitative and qualitative research on the communities health needs. It didn't stop there though. I learned Tableau in 2 days to provide an analysis of $16 million of community investment. Additionally, I was selected as one of 7 Kemper scholars in 2023 to conduct insurance research on the hispanic community.

2024

2024, my busiest year yet. For starters, my team with Enactus and I traveled to Austin to compete and present our accomplishments for the year. It was at this competition where we placed first in the nation for our community driven projects! Then to which I had the pleasure of sitting through an audit all summer...it was standard protocol. Because we were national champions, we traveled to Astana Kazakhstan to compete against Enactus national champs from 33 other countries.
